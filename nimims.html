About NMIMS CET (NCET)

The NMIMS Common Entrance Test (NCET) is the university-level examination to Undergraduate Engineering and Pharmacy degree programs. These programs are offered at NMIMS campuses in Mumbai, Indore, Navi Mumbai, Shirpur, Chandigarh, and Hyderabad.
